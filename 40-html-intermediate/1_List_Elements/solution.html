Sebastian’s Recipe for Traditional Colombian Buñuelos

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup cassava starch (almidón de yuca)
  • 1 cup costeño cheese, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 pinch of salt

For frying:

  • Vegetable oil (enough for deep frying)

Instructions

  1. Mix the cassava starch, cheese, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the egg and milk, then combine until a smooth dough forms.
  3. Roll small balls of dough with your hands.
  4. Heat the oil on medium-low — buñuelos need slow heat so they don’t burst!
  5. Fry until golden brown and perfectly round.
  6. Remove and let cool slightly. Enjoy with hot chocolate like a true Colombian!
