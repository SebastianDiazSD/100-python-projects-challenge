Sebastian’s Recipe for Traditional Colombian Buñuelos Ingredients For the dough: 1 cup cassava starch (almidón de yuca) 1 cup costeño cheese, grated 1 egg 3 tbsp sugar 2 tbsp milk 1 pinch of salt For frying: Vegetable oil (enough for deep frying) Instructions Mix the cassava starch, cheese, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Add the egg and milk, then combine until a smooth dough forms. Roll small balls of dough with your hands. Heat the oil on medium-low — buñuelos need slow heat so they don’t burst! Fry until golden brown and perfectly round. Remove and let cool slightly. Enjoy with hot chocolate like a true Colombian!
