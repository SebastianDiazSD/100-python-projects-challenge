What I Do

I work at the intersection of civil engineering and software development. As a construction supervisor on large railway infrastructure projects, I’m involved in planning, execution, and quality control on site.

In parallel, I develop software tools and data-driven solutions using Python and web technologies, with the goal of automating workflows, improving transparency, and supporting better decision-making in infrastructure projects.

Who I Am

I’m a Colombian civil engineer living and working in Germany. I grew up in Colombia, where adaptability and creativity are part of everyday problem-solving, and I now apply that mindset in structured engineering environments across Europe.

I speak Spanish (native), English, and German, and I enjoy working across cultures, disciplines, and technical domains. I’m currently deepening my computer science skills with a long-term focus on infrastructure digitalization and automation.
