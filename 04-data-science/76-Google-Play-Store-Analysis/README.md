## 📌 Overview

Welcome to my **Project 76 of 100**.

In this challenge, I analyze thousands of apps from the Google Play
Store to uncover insights about competition, pricing strategies,
downloads, and revenue potential.

Think of it like analyzing a vibrant market in Bogotá --- dynamic,
competitive, and full of opportunity. Instead of coffee stands and fruit
vendors, there are categories, installs, ratings, and pricing strategies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🎯 Project Objectives

This project answers key business questions:

-   How competitive are different app categories?
-   Which categories offer strong growth opportunities?
-   How much do downloads decrease when an app becomes paid?
-   What is a reasonable pricing range for paid apps?
-   Which paid apps generate the highest revenue?
-   Can paid apps realistically recover development costs?

## 📈 Key Insights

-   The **Games** category is highly saturated.
-   Free apps dominate total downloads.
-   Paid apps can generate strong revenue --- but only in specific
    niches.
-   Pricing strategy significantly impacts revenue potential.
-   Only a portion of paid apps generate substantial returns.

Success in the app marketplace --- just like launching a startup in
Colombia --- requires strategy, positioning, and understanding your
audience.
